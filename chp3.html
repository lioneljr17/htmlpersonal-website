Using Hyperlinks toConnect Content

The World Wide Web is often described as a network of billions of interconnected pages of content. When viewing webpages within a browser, users click on a hyperlink to move from one document to another or from one location on a page to another. A hyperlink is text or an image within a webpage that a user clicks on to jump to or display a specific page of content.
